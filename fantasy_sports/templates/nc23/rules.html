Rules and Settings

Basics

You are in the role of a Aoe2 team manager and have to field a team for the Nations Cup 2023 Tournament. On the transfer market you bet with competing managers for the players participating in the tournament. You will be awarded points for the performance of your players, the manager with the most points in the end will win.

Lineup

You can field up to four players in your lineup. If you field less, you recieve negative points. You can field two flanks and two pockets. If a player plays in the tournament on the same position as in your lineup you will get bonus points. Additionally, you can choose a captain. The captain's performance has a higher impact on your score.

The lineups for a round are locked at the first day of the round at noon (12:00) GMT, which are the following:

  • 22.02. - group stage
  • 17.03. - round of 16
  • 30.03. - quarter finals and lower bracket round 1 & 2
  • 15.04. - semifinals and lower bracket round 3 & 4
  • TBA - winners final and lower bracket round 5
  • TBA - losers final and grand final

Transfer Market

You can acquire new players on the transfer market for . For players without a manager there will be a minimum price. Your offer will be accepted if it is the highest offer that is above the minimum price. Offers will be accepted each day at midnight GMT. You can also decide to sell your players to other managers, then the prices and terms can be decided by you.

If players are out of the tournament, their name will be displayed struck through. Such a player can still be fielded in a lineup to avoid negative points. A compensation of 100 will be added to your budget, so you can bid for new players that are still in the tournament.

You may find players on the transfer market that have not qualified yet. If they become part of your team but then fail to qualify, they will be removed and you get the minimum price for the player back.
